Sherlock Holmes in
The Speckled Band

Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson are on the case in this classic mystery.

Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s stage play, based on his short story of the same name, is infused with a healthy dose of slapstick and physical humor in this new adaptation.

A damsel in distress, daring disguises, dangerous discoveries, and a deducing detective. Is it a mystery? Is it a farce? Is it a whodunit? Surely Sherlock will have the answers!

1f, 2m, 3 any gender. Dramatic Comedy, Two Acts.
'The Woman in Black' meets 'The 39 Steps'.
